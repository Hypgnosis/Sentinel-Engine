## The "2 AM" Proof: Stateless but Mindful

One of the most critical differentiators of the Sentinel Engine is its **Recursive CTE Hydration** pipeline. Unlike standard serverless agents that suffer from "context amnesia" during long-running multi-turn arbitrations, Sentinel remains **Stateless but Mindful**.

At "2 AM" (or any point of failure/restart), the system can reconstruct the entire decision tree—including parent risks, child dependencies, and sibling anomalies—using a single, high-performance recursive query. This ensures that every inference is anchored in the full lineage of the evidence locker, providing industrial-grade continuity without the overhead of persistent state clusters.
